Christmas


Cars aren't the only thing, they actually pale in comparison for his love of Christmas. Every year, we decorate almost every inch of the house with lights. For awhile he has been the designer behind it all. As of 2023 though he handed the reigns over to me and for the last 2 Christmasesnow I have designed the layout of the lights. It's probably become our biggest annual thing.


Life on the River


Once summertime rolls around, we're out on the River. We have a campground on the Rappahanock that we stay at during the Summer and Fall months. It's an amazing place, our favorite things to do is watch the sunsets and take the jetskies out on the water.



Golf Carts


Because of the campground, our main form of transportation is golf carts. He has probably worked on every single golf cart there at least once. Everyone there knows him and apprecietes him for what he does.



At the end of the season I bought my own golf cart. Over the winter this year we have been rebuilding it and I've been right along side him working on it. Hopefully it will be ready to go by March.
